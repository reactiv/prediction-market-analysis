Statistical Validation

19 pairs with sufficient T1B bar data (≥288 bars, ≥50% overlap) validated with level correlation, cross-correlation, Granger causality, and tail dependence.

Level vs Return Correlation: Price level correlation (mean 0.40) is much more meaningful than return correlation (mean 0.05) for prediction markets. Prices of related markets track each other in levels even when bar-by-bar returns are independent noise.
Data Coverage Bottleneck: Only 19 of 213 resolved pairs have sufficient bar data. The 5,832 cross-platform identical pairs from T9b are currently unvalidated because T1B bars don't cover both sides. Expanding T1B coverage would unlock the full arbitrage potential.
